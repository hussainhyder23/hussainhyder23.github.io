LETTER FROM THE CORE COMMITTEE

Esteemed Delegates, Honourable Executive Board members, and Distinguished Guests,

It gives us immense pleasure to welcome you all to the ninth edition of CBIT Model United Nations Conference, organized in collaboration with the United Nations Information Center for India and Bhutan.

After eight successful conferences that have been committed to providing the best possible experience for our delegates, CBITMUN has garnered a reputation for its top-notch debate quality, diplomacy standards and initiatives which take us beyond the spectrum of committee halls and intense debates.

With this upcoming edition, we aim to preserve the essence of the oldest MUN in Hyderabad and sustain its legacy of high quality and impeccable standards. We are also committed to expanding the diversity of our delegate pool, optimizing our committees and technical systems in order to provide the best possible delegate experience, we have become known for throughout India and beyond. The team is extremely excited to meet all of you and wishes to foster both a professional and approachable ambience focused on connecting people with similar interests and ambitions.

See you all on the 20th of September!

Regards,

About CBITMUN

Back in 2011 CBIT MUN club was started by a group of debate enthusiasts with the motto to set up, encourage and endorse Model United Nations Conferences in the city of Hyderabad. It was Model United Nations Initiative for the first time of its level in the city of Hyderabad. As years passed it has not only succeeded in conducting a world class conference every year, with a sharp increase in the number of participants with every edition. Since its inception the club has stood for the quality of debate and hospitality it delivers during the conference.
