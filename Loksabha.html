Lok Sabha

It is with great enthusiasm that we disclose that the next committee to be simulated at CBITMUN 2019 is the house of the people- The Lok Sabha. Lok Sabha is composed of representatives of the people chosen by direct election on the basis of the adult suffrage and it can exercise its legislative, executive, judicial and electoral powers to take decisions in the interest of the country's peace-keeping and development. Simulating the Lok Sabha at CBITMUN is a never-before endeavour to provide an amicable platform to discuss the predominant issues that are closer to home.
