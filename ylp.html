YOUNG LEADERS’ PROGRAM

ABOUT

Young Leaders’ Program is an initiative taken up by CBITMUN to give an opportunity to school students to be a part of the best Model United Nations conference in the country, giving them the golden moments of being a part of a conference of this scale as a member of the Executive Board sharing dais with the experienced MUNers from across the globe.

This would not only serve as a platform that would give them great exposure but would definitely boost their self-confidence and strengthen their courage to dream big. We are looking forward to have one of you talented kids on board in our journey to carry forward the legacy of this prestigious conference.

APPLICATION PROCEDURE

The procedure to apply for the Young Leaders' Program is now closed. Watch this space to find out the results.

NOTES

YLP applications are not same as the delegate applications. You must apply as a delegate to get a country allotment and be a part of the conference irrespective of the YLP selection. Not every applicant would be selected.

These are applications to be part of the EB exclusive for eighth to tenth grade students and will be judged according to the standards set for CBIT MUN 2017.
