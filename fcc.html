We're thrilled to present to you our next committee- the Futuristic Crisis Committee. The Futuristic Crisis Committee at CBITMUN 2019 is sure to put you in unfathomable scenarios and challenge your wit and spontaneity. So pack your bags and let's time travel to an era of escalated tensions. Are you diplomatic enough to resolve the crisis peacefully? We've given you every reason to stay excited for you never know what the future has in store!
